About

I am an illustrator, music creator and tech enthusiast. I love making (hopefully) beautiful things; be it music, art, stories or games… My current project is learning to create a 2D game incorporating my illustrations and my music compositions in a dream-like sequence, inspired by Jungian theories on dream analysis. I want to get better everyday at making my ideas and dreams come to life, using all the tools available with technology. My goal is to create experiences that make people feel something, give them comfort and inspiration, as my favorite artists have done for me. ❤️

    'Fun' Facts:

  • I once seriously aspired to become a mangaka and would draw everyday to fulfill that goal.
  • I ended up studying Arabic at University for 3 years under the impression it would make me employable to the MI6.
  • I still don't get the 10 Arabic verb forms. I may never do.
