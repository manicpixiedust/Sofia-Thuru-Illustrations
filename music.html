Music

Like many bedroom musicians, I started producing music as soon as I got my first macbook in my early 20s. Growing up, I sung in church and operatic choirs and trained as a classical singer. I have been exploring music production and electronic soundscapes for about 5/6 years, working mostly with vocals and music programming softwares. I am quite honestly obsessed with music and my goal is to make songs that I would enjoy drawing to. Hence, my genre is 'music to draw to'. Currently, I'm working on my first EP which I hope to share sometimes in summer. Stay... ~tuned.

Creative Coding

As of 2020, I've been learning front end coding focusing on web development and design. I'm interested in getting more into creative programming and developing interactive stories and sonic-visual experimentations. A work in progress for sure.
